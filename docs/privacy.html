Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 18, 2026

tl;dr for Parents

Kids AI Dojo (KAID) is designed to be completely safe and private.

  • 🚫We do NOT collect any personal information.
  • 🚫We do NOT use any analytics or tracking.
  • 🚫We do NOT show ads.
  • 🚫We do NOT share any sensitive data with third parties.

1. Information Collection & COPPA Compliance

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from children or adults. No sensitive data leaves the app. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

2. App Data & Data Deletion

Any data used in the app are saved securely and solely on your device. Uninstalling the app will instantly remove all saved data. Because no data is collected or transmitted to our servers, we have no accounts to delete and no user data on file to fulfill deletion requests under GDPR, CCPA, or App Store guidelines.

3. AI & Voice Features

Your child's interactions within the app are never shared with or transmitted to any third party. All voice recordings made using the Personal Voice feature stay entirely on your device and are never uploaded anywhere.

4. External Links

The app contains links to this privacy policy and support pages in the Parents Area. These links are protected by a Parental Gate to ensure children do not inadvertently access the web.
