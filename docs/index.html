The AI Generation Challenge

human skills matter more

The children growing up today need a new kind of preparation — not just academic knowledge, but the uniquely human abilities that technology can never replicate.

🤖

AI handles the routine

Memorization, calculation, and rule-following are increasingly automated. What remains irreplaceable is creativity, empathy, and human judgment.

How It Works

Four steps to skills
that last a lifetime

Every adventure follows the same proven loop — from understanding a skill to practising it for real.

1

Guided Learning

New to the app? A fun, guided walkthrough shows kids how everything works — the dojo, adventures, coaches, and rewards — so they feel confident before they begin.

2

Pick a Skill & Learn It

Choose from 9 skill categories — critical thinking, creativity, collaboration and more. Before every adventure, kids get a bite-sized intro on what the skill is, why it matters, and a real-life example.

3

Practise the Skill with Role-Play

Step into a real-world scenario with a unique coach character. Read the situation, make choices, and get instant encouraging feedback on every response.

4

Earn, Level Up & Reinforce

Earn stars and build combo streaks to unlock belt upgrades — from White Belt to Black Belt. Collect trophies, champion badges, and mastery titles like "Logic Legend." Every three adventures unlock a collectible Wisdom Card. Spend stars in the Dojo Shop on premium avatars, belt upgrades and streaks!

Everything Included

Built to make kids
love learning

Every feature is designed to keep kids engaged, motivated, and coming back for more.

📖

Skill Introductions

Every adventure begins with a kid-friendly intro — what the skill is, why it matters, and a real-life example — so kids understand what they're actually learning.

🎭

Interactive Roleplay

Kids step into real-world scenarios and choose how to respond — building instincts through guided practice, not lectures.

⚡

Instant Feedback

Every choice gets immediate, encouraging coaching that explains why one response is better — building understanding, not just habits.

🧙

9 Unique Skill Coaches

Every skill category has its own coach with a personality kids remember — Inspector Clue 🕵️, Coach Calm 🧘, Coach Bounce 🦘, Wise Fox 🦊, Builder Bo 🏗️, Captain Connect 🤝, Coach Veer 🎨, Coach Dean 💻, and Professor Query 🔬.

🥋

Dojo Trial & Boss Fight

Every adventure has two stages — conquer the Dojo Trial first, then unlock the Boss Fight for an extra challenge. Score 60%+ to prove you're ready for the boss round.

🔥

Combo Streaks & Bonus Stars

Pick great answers in a row to build a combo streak — fire animations blaze across the screen and bonus stars rain down. Kids can't stop chasing the next streak.

🥷

15 Hero Avatars

Create a hero with 9 free avatars — Ninja, Astronaut, Wizard, Fairy, and more. Unlock 6 premium avatars like Dragon, Alien, and Fox Spirit in the Dojo Shop.

🥋

Belt Progression System

Progress through 9 martial arts belts — from White Belt all the way to Black Belt. Spend your earned stars to level up and show off your rank in the Dojo.

Daily Streaks & Rewards

Play every day to build a daily streak. Hit 3+ days and earn bonus stars on new adventures. Miss a day? Spend stars to recover your streak in the Dojo Shop.

Dojo Shop

A reward marketplace where kids spend their hard-earned stars — upgrade belts, unlock premium avatars, or recover missed streaks. Every star feels valuable.
